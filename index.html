The Author

Hayk Dallakyan

In 2007, in post-Soviet Armenia with 27% unemployment, a 25-year-old engineer with a Master's degree took a job as a line operator at a chocolate factory earning $200/month. Most people would have accepted that ceiling. Hayk chose not to.

By mastering not lean tools — but people relationships — he was promoted every two years. Within 7 years he became Plant Senior Operations Director, managing 2,500+ people and increasing his income 7x.

Then he immigrated to Canada in 2017 with zero local experience, and did it again. Starting from scratch, he grew to Senior Director in less than 7 years — today managing operations generating $200M in revenue.
